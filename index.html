Welcome to Simple Translate!

The simplest graphical translation app for Ubuntu and other Linux distributions. Just type in your text in the upper half of the screen and your translation is being presented in the lower half of the screen. Simple Translate currently supports English, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Ukrainian and Dutch.

Installation

On Ubuntu, Simple Translate can be installed as follows:

  1. sudo apt-add-repository ppa:grabli66-r/ubuntu-ppa
  2. sudo apt-get update
  3. sudo apt-get install translator
